# wRTC Onboarding Tutorial

Welcome to the wRTC (Wrapped RustChain Credit) onboarding guide! This tutorial will walk you through everything you need to know about wRTC, how to acquire it, and what you can do with it.

## What is wRTC?

wRTC is the wrapped version of RTC (RustChain Credit), the native token for RustChain's AI mining ecosystem. It's built on the Solana blockchain, making it fast, cheap to transfer, and easily tradable on decentralized exchanges.

## Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Get a Solana Wallet

If you don't have a Solana wallet yet:

1. Install [Phantom Wallet](https://phantom.app/) (recommended)
2. Create a new wallet
3. **IMPORTANT:** Save your seed phrase securely and never share it
4. Add some SOL to your wallet for gas fees

### Step 2: Add wRTC to Your Wallet

wRTC Token Address: `12TAdKXxcGf6oCv4rqDz2NkgxjyHq6HQKoxKZYGf5i4X`

**Using Phantom Wallet:**
1. Open Phantom
2. Click on the wallet icon
3. Click "Add Token"
4. Paste the mint address: `12TAdKXxcGf6oCv4rqDz2NkgxjyHq6HQKoxKZYGf5i4X`
5. The wRTC token should appear in your wallet

### Step 3: Acquire wRTC

There are three main ways to get wRTC:

#### Option A: Buy on Raydium (Easiest)

1. Visit [Raydium Swap](https://raydium.io/swap/?inputMint=sol&outputMint=12TAdKXxcGf6oCv4rqDz2NkgxjyHq6HQKoxKZYGf5i4X)
2. Connect your wallet
3. Select SOL as the input token
4. Select wRTC as the output token
5. Enter the amount of SOL you want to swap
6. Click "Swap" and confirm the transaction

#### Option B: Bridge from RTC

If you have native RTC from mining:

1. Visit the [wRTC Bridge](https://bottube.ai/bridge/wrtc)
2. Connect your wallet
3. Select the amount of RTC to bridge
4. Confirm the transaction
5. wRTC will be minted to your Solana wallet

#### Option C: Mine RTC and Bridge

1. [Download RustChain Miner](https://github.com/Scottcjn/rustchain-bounties)
2. Set up and run the miner
3. Earn RTC as rewards
4. Bridge RTC to wRTC using the bridge above

### Step 4: Using wRTC

Once you have wRTC, here's what you can do:

#### Trade on DEXs

wRTC is listed on:
- [Raydium](https://raydium.io/swap/?inputMint=sol&outputMint=12TAdKXxcGf6oCv4rqDz2NkgxjyHq6HQKoxKZYGf5i4X)
- [Jupiter Aggregator](https://jup.ag) (best rates across DEXs)
- Coming soon to other Solana DEXs

#### Bridge to BoTTube Credits

Convert wRTC to BoTTube credits for content creator tipping:

1. Visit [BoTTube Bridge](https://bottube.ai/bridge/wrtc)
2. Connect your wallet
3. Select "wRTC ‚Üí BoTTube Credits"

5. Confirm transaction
6. Use credits to tip your favorite creators on BoTTube!

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What's the difference between RTC and wRTC?

**A:** RTC is the native RustChain token. wRTC is the wrapped version on Solana, making it faster and easier to trade. You can bridge between them 1:1.

### Q: Is wRTC a stablecoin?

**A:** No, wRTC is not a stablecoin. Its value is determined by supply and demand on the open market.

### Q: What can I do with BoTTube credits?

**A:** BoTTube credits are used to tip content creators on BoTTube, an AI-generated video platform. Credits can be earned by providing compute power to the network.

### Q: How do I verify I'm using the correct wRTC token?

**A:** Always verify the mint address: `12TAdKXxcGf6oCv4rqDz2NkgxjyHq6HQKoxKZYGf5i4X`. Cross-check with official channels.

## Troubleshooting

### Token not showing in wallet?

1. Double-check the mint address
2. Make sure your wallet is connected to Solana mainnet
3. Try refreshing your wallet
4. Contact support on [Twitter](https://x.com/RustchainPOA)

### Transaction failed?

1. Check if you have enough SOL for gas fees
2. Try increasing the slippage tolerance
3. Ensure your wallet has sufficient balance
4. Check Solana network status on [Solana Beach](https://solanabeach.io)

### Bridge not working?

1. Clear your browser cache
2. Try a different browser
3. Disconnect and reconnect your wallet
4. Check [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/Scottcjn/rustchain-bounties) for known problems
